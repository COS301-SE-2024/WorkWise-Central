What is WorkWise Central?

WorkWise Central is a cutting-edge Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to revolutionise service-based industries like building, plumbing, electrical, etc. It is a comprehensive system comprising features such as employee, client job and inventory management which improves the productivity of the company. The system has been designed to be as malleable as possible allowing the businesses to shape the system to fit their needs.

Our project management

We are following the Agile approach

Our project is divided into sprints, each sprint is roughly 2 weeks long.

Each sprint consists of sprint planning, daily stand-ups, development and sprint review and retrospectives

Sprint planning, review and retrospectives

We make use of a combination of google docs and github boards.

At the end of every sprint we will review the work done and create a backlog of anything that was not fully completed (on google docs). After which we conducted the retrospective

After the review and retrospective we plan for the next sprint, where we always prioritise anything on the backlog from the previous sprint. This is also gone on google docs

After these meetings, everything from the planning meeting is made into issues and allocated to the team members.

Project Boards

Visit GitHub

We use github boards for our project management. It was chosen for the following reasons

  • Free to use
  • Integrates with all the activity on github
  • Reduces the spead of 'information' in the team. Having a separate platform mean everyone needs to keep track of that as well
  • Has the functionality we need.

Client meeting

We aim to have 1 meeting with our clients every week.

This meeting tends to be on Thursdays.

The purpose of the meeting is to give the client an update on the progress of the project and to get feedback from the client.

We also have ad hoc meeting with the client as needed.

Mentor meeting

We have a weekly meeting with our mentor, Liam.

This meeting is to get feedback from him and to get guidance on the project.

In addition to this we contact him for any clarification we may need.

Daily stand-ups

Our team has a daily stand-up meeting at 8:30 every day.

Discord

We also make use of a Discord server for communication and ad hoc online meetings
