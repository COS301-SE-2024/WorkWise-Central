What is WorkWise Central?

It is a progressive web app for small to medium-sized service delivery businesses. The project goal is to create a system that will revolutionise the way these businesses manage themselves. The project aims to address the lack of tools available to these businesses.

Our project management

We are following the Agile approach

Our project is divided into sprints, each sprint is roughly 2 weeks long.

Each sprint consists of sprint planning, daily stand-ups, development and sprint review

Sprint planning

Our sprint planning is done using our Github project board.

Everything that needs to be done in the print is added as an issue and given the relivant dates, priorities, size and allocated to a member.

As the spring goes along any changes to what needs to be done is updated in on our board.

Daily stand-ups

Our team has a daily stand up meeting at 8:30 every day.

Client meeting

We have at least 1 meeting with our clients every week.

This meeting tends to be on Thursdays.

The purpose of the meeting is to give the client an update on the progress of the project and to get feedback from the client.

We also have ad hoc meeting with the client as needed.

Mentor meeting

We have a weekly meeting with our mentor, Liam.

This meeting is to get feedback from him and to get guidance on the project.

In addition to this we contact him for any clarification we may need.

Discord

We also make use of a Discord server for communication as well as and ad hoc online meetings
